1 Read and match. making a cake, playing cards, writing a letter, doing a jigsaw, drawing a picture, reading a comic Read sentences and recognize different activities.
2 Listen and order. making a cake, playing cards, writing a letter, doing a jigsaw, drawing a picture, reading a comic Listen to a song. Understand the lyrics to put the pictures in the correct order.
3 Ask and answer. What's he / she doing? He's / She's ..., washing up, writing, drawing, watching TV, listening to music Make sentences (questions and answers) about what people are doing.
4 Listen and sing. Write. I can ..., I can't ..., play tennis, play football, ride a bike, play basketball, rollerblade, skateboard Read and sing a song. Complete sentences with I can or I can't.
5 Order the questions. Answer. Can you ...? Yes, I can, No, I can't, jump, speak English, fly, cook, play computer games Order words to make questions. Answer the questions using can.
