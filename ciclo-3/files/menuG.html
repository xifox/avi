1 Read and match. map, zebra crossing, traffic lights, underground, pavement, bridge, bench Read and understand the names of urban features.
2 Play. Match pictures and words. swimming pool, theatre, hospital, market, restaurant, shops Play a memory game with places in a town.
3 Read and follow the directions. straight on, turn left / right Understand written directions and complete sentences using prepositions of place.
4 Order the sentences. going to, sports centre, library, park, internet café, cinema, museum Order words to make sentences about places in a town with going to.
5 Listen, read and sing. there is, there are, sports centre, library, park, internet café, cinema, museum, shops Listen to a song and understand the places in a town. Sing the song.
6 Write about your town. there is, there are, there isn’t, there aren’t, sports centre, cinema, park, museum, internet café, library Write about what there is (and isn’t) in the pupils’ own town.
