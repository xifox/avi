Look and write.
The lion is .................. two tigers.
The whale is ................. the seal.
The giraffe is ................ the elephant.
The anteater is ................ the zebra and the iguana.
The lion is ................ the anteater.
  • Look at the map of the zoo with the class and talk about where the different animals are.
  • Pupils write the missing prepositions (using an IWB pen) to complete the sentences, eg, The lion is between two tigers.
