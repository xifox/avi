1 Play. Match. Do you like ...? I like ..., I don’t like ..., doing homework, dancing, going shopping, playing computer games, watching films, having picnics Play a memory game with sentences expressing likes and dislikes.
2 Find verbs. Write. play chess, write stories, buy clothes, send text messages, watch sports on TV, go swimming Find verbs in a word search and use them to complete the names of different activities.
3 Read and match. walk the dog, go shopping, play the guitar, ride a mountain bike, watch cartoons, take photos, listen to CDs, meet friends, do judo, read a book, the days of the week Recognize different activities and the days of the week.
4 Look. Complete the sentences. do judo, watch TV, go shopping, the days of the week Complete sentences about weekend activities.
5 Read and complete. What are you going to do? I’m going to …, write a story, go shopping, watch TV, send a message, play chess, go swimming Complete questions and answers using going to.
6 Listen and complete. Sing. drop litter, pick flowers, light fires, close gates, respect, destroy, protect, forest Listen and complete a song. Sing the song together.
7 Read and match. Don’t ..., drop litter, pick flowers, light fires, close the gate, recycle, use the bins Read and understand instructions about how to protect the environment.
